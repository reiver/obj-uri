obj-URI

obj-URI are acct-URI like URIs for content.

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

It is probably easiest to understand the need for obj-URI by talk about acct-URI.

acct-URI are a way of representing accounts. The “acct” in “acct-URI” is short for “account”. Most of the time these accounts are (human) user accounts. But, an account can also represent: an application, a service, a group of people, etc, in addition to representing a single person.

The reason acct-URI exist is — is to have a way of representing an accout as a URI. The reason being — there are a lot of Web and Internet technologies that expect a URI.
